Eva Chen

Filler Words

Charcoal, Marker

This artwork is inspired by the words that make me, me. I have always struggled with speaking to others without sounding awkward and I tend to use filler words like um and uh as a "shield," but it also contributes to my struggles. So, I depicted these filler words falling down on me as I look up in distress in my artwork.

As an artist, I strive to use my ability to tell stories, express my experiences in life, and bring awareness to certain issues.
